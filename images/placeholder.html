Free Image Resources

Here are some websites where you can find high-quality, free stock images:

  • Unsplash - Osaka Skyline - Free high-quality images
  • Pexels - Osaka - Free stock photos
  • Pixabay - Osaka - Free images and royalty-free stock
  • Unsplash - Japanese Office - Free office images
  • Pexels - Modern Office - Free office stock photos

How to Save the Image

  1. Find an appropriate image from one of the resources above
  2. Download the image in the highest quality available
  3. Rename the file to either "hero.jpg" or "office.jpg"
  4. Place the files in the "images" directory of your website

Image Optimization Tips

  • Compress your images to reduce file size without losing quality using tools like TinyPNG
  • Make sure the images are in JPG format for photos (or PNG if transparency is needed)
  • Consider the aspect ratio when cropping images to fit the designated areas
